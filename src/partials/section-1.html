How the service work
Your digital wallet receives rental income based on your investment amount, which you can withdraw or reinvest as desired. Rental returns are usually disbursed according to the tenancy agreement's specified conditions.
Our 100-point tool screens and verifies investment properties for top-notch opportunities. We use a third-party valuator to validate our evaluation for added assurance.
Once fully funded, we create an SPV in the Indonesia International Financial Centre for the property, designating you and your co-investors as official owners.
Although the suggested holding period for real estate investments is 2-3 years, you can exit your investment anytime by listing your shares in the share transfer or calling for a vote to sell the property.
After the sale, the income is distributed among all shareholders of the Special Purpose Vehicle (SPV). After that, participants can use the profits to buy new objects or take them from the platform to their bank account
